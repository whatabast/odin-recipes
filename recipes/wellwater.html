Recipe for Stealing Well Water

Mimir's Well

Robert Engels (1866-1920). - Lange, Adolf (1903). Deutsche Götter- und Heldensagen. B. G. Teubner, Leipzig. Page 44. Digitized version from Google Books

For this recipe, you are going to need:

  • Magical cunning and a pentient for not giving a f*ck
  • Two eyes (do this before the Eye Recipe)
  • An over friendly god in control of a Well of Knowledge
  • Not to be completely tone deaf

Directions:

  1. Practice poetry or singing
  2. Use newfound skill to get Mimir to let you near his well
  3. Drink water from well
  4. Take a couple of days off to get used to knowing everything
