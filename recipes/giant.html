Recipe for Preparing Giant

Fire or Frost

For this recipe, you are going to need:

  • A big hammer or spear
  • Magic powers
  • Super strong arms and legs
  • A pentient for blood and war
  • Also, strong teeth, as giant meat is realy tough

Directions:

  1. Sharpen your waepons and drink a whole bunch
  2. Go kill some giants for fun
  3. Gut and clean
  4. For frost giants, roast over a spit for several days
  5. For fire giants, dig right in! They are perfect straight off the spear
  6. Steal their children and lie to them about it
