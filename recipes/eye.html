How to Prepare Eye of Me (Odin)

Sourced from Wikipedia.org/odin

This is a very delicate process, so let us jump right into it.

For this recipe, you are going to need:

  • At least one eye (preferably still in your head)
  • Two ravens, although one will do in a pinch
  • Nine days and nights to spare

This is a long simmering recipe.

Directions:

  1. Get your two talking ravens in a cage nearby
  2. Hang on a tree for nine days and nights
  3. Make sure your ravens have no food, this is super important!
  4. Let the ravens out and one of them will for sure peck out one of your eyes

And that is pretty well it. Now you should be all-knowing.
