Recipe for Dead Crow

Actual Consumption Optional

By Ian Kirk from Broadstone, Dorset, UK - Carrion CrowUploaded by snowmanradio, CC BY 2.0, https://commons.wikimedia.org/w/index.php?curid=31096061

For this recipe, you are going to need:

  • A bow and arrow (can be subbed for a spear)
  • Carrion of some sort, preferably a few days old
  • Hot weather, to get the rot going strong

Directions:

  • Set dead carcus out in sun to rot
  • Wait patiently for crows to arrive
  • Middle of day two, start picking them off with weapon of choice
  • Alternative: Add poison to carcus
